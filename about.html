About

Eat! is a libary of recieps from a range of cousines and dietry requirmenets. Simply enter the dish you like to make and select any dietry requirements necessary. Click GO and your're ready to start cooking.


Eat! uses Third Party API's to aid with the function of the site as well as provide nutritional information. API's also help with filtering dietry requirements on user searched reciepes.
